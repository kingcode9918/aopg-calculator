# AOPG Calculator

A calculator for **A One Piece Game** on Roblox.

## Features

- **Build Calculator**: Calculate your character's stats based on base stats, ghost stats, and accessories
- **Accessory Viewer**: Browse and compare accessories with their stat bonuses
- **Buff Calculator**: View race buffs and their multipliers
- **Move Calculator**: Calculate damage for different moves

## Stats

- **Strength**: Physical damage stat
- **Defense**: Damage reduction stat
- **Sword**: Sword damage stat
- **Special**: Special/Fruit damage stat

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the AOPG Calculator from the home page
2. Use the navigation menu to switch between Build, Accessory, Buff, and Move tabs
3. Input your stats and select your equipment to see calculated results
